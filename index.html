01Overview

We started inCybe with a simple goal — make tech easier for people.

A collaboration between inCybe and Giblets Creations. An OS that collaborates with you, organises itself, and evolves over time. With our guerrilla development style, we attack problems head on and produce revolutionary results.

The project is grounded in the Giblets Creations stack — proven tooling, mobile-first on the S24 Ultra, and a philosophy of building what you need rather than buying what exists.
